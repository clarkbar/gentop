Let \(X \subseteq \RR^n\) be a subspace. For any family \(\{Z_a\}_{a\in A}\) of closed subsets of \(X\text{,}\) the intersection \(\bigcap_{a\in A} Z_a\) is closed as well; if \(A\) is finite, then the union \(\bigcup_{a\in A} Z_a\) is closed as well. Dually, for any family \(\{U_a\}_{a\in A}\) of open subsets of \(X\text{,}\) the union \(\bigcup_{a\in A} U_a\) is open as well; if \(A\) is finite, then the intersection \(\bigcap_{a\in A} U_a\) is open as well.
