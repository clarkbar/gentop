Proposition 2.2.10.

Let \(X\) and \(Y\) be topological spaces, and let \(x \in X\text{.}\) The following are equivalent for a map \(f \colon X \to Y\text{.}\)

  1. The map \(f\) is continuous at \(x\text{.}\)

  2. For any open neighborhood \(V\) of \(f(x)\text{,}\) the inverse image \(f^{-1}(V)\) is an open neighborhood of \(x\text{.}\)
