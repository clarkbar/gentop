Let \(W = \bigcap_{a\in A} Z_a\text{.}\) We aim to prove that \(\tau_X(W) \subseteq W\text{.}\) Let \(x \in X\) be a point that is close to \(W\text{.}\) For every \(a \in A\text{,}\) since \(W \subseteq Z_a\text{,}\) it follows that \(x\) is close to \(Z_a\) as well. Since \(Z_a\) is closed, \(x \in Z_a\text{.}\) This happens for every \(a \in A\text{,}\) so \(x \in W\text{.}\)
