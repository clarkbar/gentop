Please notice that this notation is a little sloppy. The fiber product \(U \times_X V\) depends on the maps \(f\) and \(g\text{.}\) If one needs to make the choice of maps explicit, one may write

\begin{equation*} U \operatorname{\times}_{f,X,g} V\text{,} \end{equation*}

which is a bit busy, but very clear!
