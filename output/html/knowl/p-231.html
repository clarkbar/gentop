Let \(S_1, \dots, S_n \subseteq \PP^1_{\RR}\) be a collection of subsets of \(\PP^1_{\RR}\text{,}\) and let \(L \in \PP^1_{\RR}\text{.}\) If \(L\) is close to \(S_1 \cup \cdots \cup S_n\text{,}\) then a Pigeonhole argument like the one given in Proposition 1.1.4 ensures that \(L\) is close to \(S_1 \cup \cdots \cup S_n\text{.}\)
