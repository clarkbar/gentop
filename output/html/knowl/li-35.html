Item.

If we have a finite collection of subspaces \(X_1 \subseteq \RR^{n_1}, \dots, X_k \subseteq \RR^{n_k}\) then we obtain the subspace

\begin{equation*} X_1 \times \cdots \times X_k \coloneq \{ x = (x^1, \dots, x^k) \in \RR^{n_1} \times \cdots \times \RR^{n_k} : (\forall i)(x^i \in X_i) \}\text{.} \end{equation*}
